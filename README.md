Overview
This project analyzes the operations and performance of Bright Coffee Shop using data insights. The goal is to better understand sales trends, customer preferences, and business efficiency in order to support smarter decision-making.

The analysis focuses on identifying patterns in revenue, product performance, peak hours, and customer behavior.

Objectives
Evaluate overall sales performance
Identify best-selling and underperforming products
Analyze customer purchasing trends
Determine peak business hours and days
Provide actionable recommendations for growth

Dataset
The dataset includes transactional and operational data such as:

Order dates and times
Product categories and items
Sales revenue
Quantity sold

Key Analysis Areas
1. Sales Performance
Daily, weekly, and monthly revenue trends
Revenue distribution across products
2. Product Insights
Top-selling items
Least popular products
Category-level performance
3. Customer Behavior
Buying patterns
Order frequency
Average transaction value
4. Time-Based Trends
Peak hours of operation
Busy vs slow days
Seasonal variations

Recommendations
Optimize staffing during peak hours
Promote or rework underperforming products
Introduce bundles or upselling strategies
Adjust inventory based on demand patterns

Future Improvements
Incorporate customer segmentation
Add predictive analytics for demand forecasting
Include external factors (weather, holidays, location trends)
Build an interactive dashboard
